How to make a PB&J sandwich in 7 steps

  1. Take two slices of bread
  2. Throw them in the toaster
  3. Take them out the toaster when ready
  4. Grab Peanut Butter (i prefer Jiffy Peanut butter personally)
  5. Spread Peanut Butter on both pieces of toast (This is the trick to keeping the bread from sogging, you're welcome)
  6. Next, spread out jelly/preserve/jam (I prefer some blackcurrant preserve, or raspberry or even blackberry)
  7. Put the toast pieces together
  8. Enjoy...yumm
